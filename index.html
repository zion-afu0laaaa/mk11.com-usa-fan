First, some History of MORTAL KOMBAT

Made by Ed Boon, Dan Forden, John Edwards, Paulo Garcia, and John Tobias, Mortal Kombat originated in the year 1992, released August 9th. It got very popular because of the blood and gore(which often splattered across the screen whenever you hit your opponent or when you got hit) It was also the first fighting game to have secret character, and a secret character select screen.
